##### What is Knowledge Base ?


##### Knowledge Base là gì ?
- **Knowledge Base** is "private memory" for Agent to access your specialized information, which the original AI model does not know.

1. On the interface page of **Aws Bedrock**, I choose the left side **Knowledge Base**

![alt text](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-S3-onprem/kb1.png)

2. When you have finished configuring, wait about 5 minutes for it to initialize.

![alt text](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-S3-onprem/kb2.png)

- This has created the Knowledge base.
>*Note that you should configure it according to your needs and grant it the necessary permissions*
